DOLL HOUSE

"Doll House" explores the concept of voyeurism through scenes of surreal domesticity at night. Produced across two states and in twelve homes, it is a phantasmogorical illustration of privacy in the twenty-first century and through this, it attempts to define the fabricated lives that we live.



Associated Exhibition with Head On photo festival 2016

Breathing Colours Gallery, Balmain, NSW, Australia
